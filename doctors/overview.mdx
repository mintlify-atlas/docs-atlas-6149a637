## Key Benefits

Aria empowers doctors to focus on patient care by automating administrative tasks and providing intelligent clinical support.

### Reduce Administrative Burden

Spend less time on paperwork and more time with patients. Our AI-powered dictation and smart medication search streamline prescription creation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Speak naturally">
    Dictate prescriptions in your own words - no need for structured formats
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic recognition">
    AI identifies medications, dosages, symptoms, and medical terminology
  </Step>
  <Step title="Instant prescription">
    Digital prescription created and sent to patient's ABHA account
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Enhanced Diagnosis with Patient History

Make informed decisions with instant access to complete patient medical records through ABHA integration.

<Info>
  **ABHA Integration**: All prescriptions and medical records are automatically linked to patients' ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) IDs, ensuring seamless access across healthcare providers.
</Info>

<Accordion title="What information is available in patient history?">
  - All previous prescriptions from any ABHA-linked doctor
  - Lab reports and test results
  - Imaging reports (X-rays, CT scans, MRI)
  - Chronic conditions and active medications
  - Known drug allergies
  - Uploaded medical documents from patients
</Accordion>

### AI-Powered Clinical Insights

Get intelligent diagnosis suggestions based on patient complaints cross-referenced with their complete medical history.

<Warning>
  AI diagnosis suggestions are meant to assist clinical decision-making, not replace it. Always use your professional judgment when making final diagnoses.
</Warning>

### Streamlined Patient Communication

Digital prescriptions are automatically delivered to patients through their ABHA account, with instant notifications.

<Note>
  Patients can access their prescriptions anytime on their mobile devices, reducing calls for prescription clarification.
</Note>

## ABHA & ABDM Integration

Aria is fully integrated with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) ecosystem.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="What is ABHA?">
    ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) is a unique health ID for every Indian citizen that enables secure access to medical records across healthcare providers.
    
    [Learn more about ABHA](https://abha.abdm.gov.in/abha/v3)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="How It Works">
    1. Patients link their ABHA ID to Aria
    2. You create prescriptions linked to their ABHA account
    3. Records are automatically synced with the national health stack
    4. Patients can share their complete history with any ABDM-linked provider
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Benefits">
    - No duplicate patient records
    - Seamless continuity of care across providers
    - Government-mandated compliance
    - National-level health data portability
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
