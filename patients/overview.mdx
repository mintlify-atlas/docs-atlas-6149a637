## Key Benefits

<Info>
  **Clarity Across Your Care** - Aria provides complete visibility into your health journey, ensuring you and your healthcare providers have access to accurate, up-to-date medical information.
</Info>

### Access Your Complete Medical History

Your medical records are available 24/7 from any device. Whether you're visiting a new doctor, traveling, or need to reference past treatments, your complete health history is just a tap away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Complete Timeline">
    See all your prescriptions, lab reports, and medical records in one chronological timeline
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and Filter">
    Quickly find specific prescriptions, diagnoses, or treatments from your entire medical history
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Anywhere">
    Use the Aria mobile app or web portal to access your records from any location
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Never Lose Important Documents

All your medical documents are stored securely in the cloud and backed up automatically. No more lost prescriptions, misplaced lab reports, or missing records.

<Note>
  Every prescription your doctor creates in Aria is automatically saved to your account. You'll never need to worry about losing a paper prescription again.
</Note>

### Easy Sharing with Healthcare Providers

Share your medical records with any doctor or hospital instantly. Whether you're getting a second opinion, visiting a specialist, or traveling, your healthcare providers can access your complete medical history with your permission.

### Track Your Health Digitally

Monitor your medications, treatment progress, and health trends over time. Aria helps you stay organized and informed about your healthcare journey.

<Tip>
  Upload old medical records, lab reports, and hospital documents to digitize your complete health history in one place.
</Tip>

## ABHA Integration

Aria is integrated with India's Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) system, ensuring your medical records are linked to your national health ID for seamless healthcare across India.

## Getting Started

Ready to take control of your healthcare journey? Here's how to get started with Aria:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Aria Patient App">
    Install the Aria app from the App Store or Google Play Store
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up with your mobile number and link your ABHA ID (or create one during registration)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Existing Records">
    Digitize your old prescriptions, lab reports, and medical documents by uploading photos or scans
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit Your Doctor">
    When your doctor uses Aria to create prescriptions, they'll automatically appear in your account
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You Can Do

### For Current Treatments

- View digital prescriptions from all your doctors
- Track medication schedules and dosages
- Set reminders for taking medications
- Monitor treatment progress and outcomes

### For Medical Records

- Upload and store lab reports, X-rays, and scans
- Digitize old paper prescriptions and hospital records
- Organize documents by date, doctor, or condition
- Search your entire medical history instantly

### For Healthcare Visits

- Share relevant medical history with new doctors
- Provide complete information for emergency care
- Show medication history to pharmacists
- Access records during telemedicine consultations

<Note>
  **Privacy First** - You control who sees your medical information. Share only what you choose, when you choose.
</Note>
