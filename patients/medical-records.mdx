## Complete Medical History at Your Fingertips

Aria gives you a comprehensive timeline of your entire medical history. View prescriptions, lab reports, imaging results, and doctor's notes in one organized, chronological view.

<Info>
  Your medical records are stored securely in the cloud and automatically backed up. They're accessible 24/7 from any device with your Aria account.
</Info>

## Your Patient Timeline

The patient timeline is the heart of your medical record management. It shows every medical event in chronological order, making it easy to track your health journey over time.

### What's Included in Your Timeline

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Digital Prescriptions" icon="prescription">
    Every prescription created by your doctors using Aria
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lab Reports" icon="flask">
    Blood tests, pathology reports, and laboratory results
  </Card>
  <Card title="Imaging" icon="x-ray">
    X-rays, CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasound reports
  </Card>
  <Card title="Medical Documents" icon="file-medical">
    Hospital discharge summaries, doctor's notes, and consultation records
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Viewing Your Timeline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Your Medical Records">
    Launch the Aria app and tap on **Medical Records** from the home screen
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse by Date">
    Your timeline displays all records in reverse chronological order, with the most recent at the top
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap to Expand">
    Click on any record to see complete details, including diagnosis, medications, dosages, and doctor's notes
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download or Share">
    Use the share button to send records to other healthcare providers or download PDFs for your files
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uploading Medical Records

Digitize your paper records and old medical documents by uploading them to Aria. This creates a complete, centralized health history.

<Note>
  **Supported Formats** - Upload photos (JPEG, PNG), PDFs, and scanned documents. The app automatically optimizes and stores them securely.
</Note>

### How to Upload Documents

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Upload">
    In the Aria app, tap the **+** button or go to **Medical Records > Upload Document**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Document Type">
    Select the type of document you're uploading: Lab Report, Prescription, Imaging, or Other Medical Document
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take Photo or Select File">
    Either take a photo of the physical document or select an existing file from your device
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details">
    Enter the date of the record, doctor's name, and any relevant notes to make it easier to find later
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload and Save">
    Tap **Upload** to securely save the document to your medical timeline
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  **Best Practices for Scanning**
  - Use good lighting when photographing documents
  - Ensure text is clear and readable
  - Capture the entire document in the frame
  - Upload high-resolution images for better quality
</Tip>

## Organizing Your Records

Aria automatically organizes your medical records by date, but you can also search, filter, and categorize them for easy access.

### Search Your Medical History

Use the search function to instantly find specific records:

- Search by **doctor's name** to see all consultations with a specific physician
- Search by **condition or diagnosis** to track treatment for a specific health issue
- Search by **medication name** to find all prescriptions containing that drug
- Search by **date range** to review your health during a specific period

### Filter by Type

Quickly filter your timeline to show only specific types of records:

## Understanding Prescription Details

When you tap on a prescription in your timeline, you'll see comprehensive information:

### Prescription Information Includes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Diagnosis">
    The condition or symptoms diagnosed by your doctor
  </Step>
  <Step title="Medications">
    Complete list of prescribed medications with:
    - **Medication name** and strength (e.g., Paracetamol 500mg)
    - **Dosage instructions** (e.g., 1 tablet twice daily after meals)
    - **Duration** of treatment (e.g., 5 days)
    - **Special instructions** or warnings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Doctor Details">
    Name, specialty, and contact information of the prescribing physician
  </Step>
  <Step title="Date and Time">
    When the prescription was created
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **ABHA-Linked Records** - Prescriptions created through Aria are automatically linked to your ABHA health record, making them accessible across the entire ABDM network in India.
</Info>

## Track Treatment Progress

Monitor your health journey over time by reviewing your medical timeline.

### See Treatment Patterns

- Identify recurring symptoms or conditions
- Track medication changes over time
- Monitor lab values and test results
- Review treatment effectiveness

### Medication History

View your complete medication history to:

- See what medications you've taken in the past
- Identify patterns in your prescriptions
- Avoid medication conflicts

<Tip>
  **For Chronic Conditions** - If you have ongoing health conditions, your medical timeline helps you and your doctors see how treatments have evolved and track long-term progress.

## Downloading and Printing

Need a physical copy of your records? Aria makes it easy to download and print.

### Download Options

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Record">
    Open any prescription or medical record from your timeline
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Download">
    Click the download icon to save a PDF copy to your device
  </Step>
  <Step title="Print or Share">
    Open the PDF and print it or share it via email, WhatsApp, or other apps
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Downloaded PDFs include all prescription details, doctor information, and a QR code for verification. They're accepted by pharmacies and healthcare providers.
</Note>

## Privacy and Security

Your medical records are protected with enterprise-grade security.

### Security Features

- **ABDM compliance** - meets national health data security standards
- **Audit logs** - track who has accessed your records and when
